About Go Viz

During the timer, examine the stones and remember their color and location.

When the timer ends, repeat the same pattern.

Single press for a white stone, press again for a black stone, press again to clear.

Hit Check to see how you did.

Double-tap anywhere on the screen to interrupt and speed up the timer.

Progress through all the levels.

Improve your memory and have fun!
